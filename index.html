 Lensified since 2005


Lensified is the official photography club of Netaji Subhash Engineering College. Lensified started its journey in the 2005 and has some glorious years till date. Our Objectives

  • Lensified aims at providing the platform for budding photographers.
  • Lensified believes in offering the best hands-on photography experience.
  • Lensified believes in teaching photography to anyone who is interested to learn.
  • Lensified does not think device to be a barrier. No matter which device you use, whether it is a phone or camera, you can still achieve your photography dreams.


Lensified's Events

All around the year, Lensified organises a number of events where students are encouraged to dive deeper into their passion of photography. Some events include Consequat, Hasta La Vista, Photowalks, Forums and a lot more. Apart from its own events, it also collaborates with various organizational bodies like Rotaract Club of Calcutta Diamond, IEEE, Sports Club, etc. Such collaborative events include Dugga Dugga, Icchedana, Zest, Infinito, CALCON, and many more.
